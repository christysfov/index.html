💡 The BA Formula Corner:
The standard error is calculated as SE = √( p_A(1 - p_A)/n_A + p_B(1 - p_B)/n_B ).
The Z-Score is calculated as Z = (p_B - p_A) / SE.
Our $p$-value represents the probability that this conversion difference could occur purely due to background noise. If $p \le 0.05$, we reject the Null Hypothesis and confidently rollout the update.
